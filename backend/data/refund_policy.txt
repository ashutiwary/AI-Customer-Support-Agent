REFUND POLICY

Rule 1:
Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of purchase.

Rule 2:
Items marked as Final Sale are not eligible for refunds.

Rule 3:
Customers with more than 5 refunds in the last year require Manual Review.

Rule 4:
Products damaged during delivery qualify for refunds.

Rule 5:
Digital products are not refundable.

Rule 6:
Refund amount cannot exceed original purchase amount.
